About Me

My name is Ryan Wallie, and I am a committed Full Stack System Developer, Technician, and emerging Systems Engineer driven by a passion for understanding, building, and refining technology. My journey in the world of IT began with a natural curiosity for how systems operate both on the surface and behind the scenes. Over the years, that curiosity evolved into a disciplined pursuit of technical excellence, continuous learning, and meaningful innovation.
I have completed formal training in System Development and IT Support through institutions such as CAPACITI and the IIE Rosebank College, and I am currently advancing my expertise as a Systems Engineer at WeThinkCode. These experiences have shaped me into a versatile professional with strong foundations in programming, networking, system installations, and full-stack development. From building responsive web applications to supporting users through complex technical challenges, I strive to create solutions that are not only functional, but impactful and future-ready.


Creativity plays an essential role in my approach to technology. Whether I am refining a user interface, optimizing a piece of code, or solving a technical issue, I enjoy transforming challenges into opportunities for improvement. I believe that great technology is built through a combination of logic, experimentation, and thoughtful design—elements that I bring into every project. My professional experience spans both the technical and operational sides of IT. At CAPACITI, I gained hands-on exposure to software development practices, teamwork, and real-world problem-solving. During my time at Clicks, I strengthened my ability to work efficiently, communicate effectively, and support day-to-day operations—all skills that complement my technical foundation.
I am fluent in multiple programming languages, familiar with various operating systems, and confident working across hardware and software environments. More importantly, I am committed to staying curious and adaptable in a field that evolves every day. I value collaboration, continuous improvement, and the opportunity to contribute to technology that makes a difference. As I continue developing my craft, my goal is to become a well-rounded engineer who builds reliable, thoughtful, and innovative solutions. I am eager to connect with teams and individuals who share a passion for technology, creativity, and long-term impact.
